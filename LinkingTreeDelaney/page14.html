Spittin' some honey facts:

Honey's ingredients include:
1. nectar from flowers
2. enzymes added by the bees

Bees then fan the nectar, evaporating it into honey.

One bee alone contributes 1/8 teaspoon in their lifetime.

Bees travel around the equivalent of two and a half times around the world to create one pound of honey.
