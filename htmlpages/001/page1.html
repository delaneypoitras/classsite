This HTML file explores the header tag.



As the number for the header tag increases, up to 6,



the size of the font decreases.



This quick poem will soon come



to a close when the tag reaches


the number 6.


There's comfort in staying and understanding


the place that you reside.


To grow again wouldn't be so bad either...


To feel like the expansion is for the better.



Knowing that you're going back to your highest point.



But... then... you get back to a place where you can't grow.



And maybe you feel stagnant for a bit.



You feel like you aren't getting worse,



But you aren't getting better.



Finally you do get better, though!



You grow. And keep growing.



The header tag is different from the "bold" tag, however.

You can tell here when the tag is used.

They do look quite similar but like everything else in the world, they are different.
